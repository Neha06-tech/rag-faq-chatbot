What is your return policy?
You can return any unopened product within 30 days for a full refund. Opened products may be eligible for store credit depending on the condition. To start a return, visit our Returns portal or contact support at support@ourbrand.com.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout and usually arrive within 1-3 business days.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we ship to many countries. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes.

How do I track my order?
After your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also log into your account on our website and visit "Orders" to see the latest tracking status.

What materials are your products made from?
Our core products are made from sustainably sourced cotton and recycled polyester. Each product page lists detailed material information and care instructions.

How can I contact customer support?
You can reach customer support via email at support@ourbrand.com or through the chat widget on our website. Our support hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm ET.

Do you offer discounts for subscriptions?
Yes — subscribe & save options are available for select products. Subscriptions typically offer 10–20% off and can be managed from your account dashboard.

Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be changed or canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that window, we may have already started processing. Contact support as soon as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. We also support buy-now-pay-later options through our payment partners on eligible orders.

How do I care for my product?
Follow the care instructions on the product label. Generally, machine wash cold with like colors, tumble dry low, and avoid bleach to preserve color and fabric quality.
